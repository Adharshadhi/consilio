Guidelines

Prevent the spread of COVID19

Keep at least 6 feet between yourself and others if you are in public.

Wear a cloth face covering over your mouth and nose when around others.

Cover your nose and mouth with a tissue or use your elbow.

Wash your hands often with soap and water for at least 20 seconds.

Clean and disinfect surfaces around your home and work frequently.

Take both doses of vaccine as soon as possible.

Schedule vaccine for COVID19

Go to Cowin portal and register using your mobile number.

Provide the OTP required for the registration from the mobile.

Fill the required details for registering yourself or other members.

After registring, schedule an appointment by searching available slots.

Check properly your age, vaccination center and available vaccine.

Choose a time period and confirm the details for appointment.
